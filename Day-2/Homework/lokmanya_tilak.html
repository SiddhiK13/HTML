Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak

Lokmanya Tilak (1856–1920) was an Indian nationalist, teacher, and independence activist. He was one of the first leaders of the Indian Independence Movement.

Famous Quote:

"Swaraj is my birthright and I shall have it!"
