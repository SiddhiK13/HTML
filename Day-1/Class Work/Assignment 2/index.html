A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015)

Bio Works Quotes Contact

Biography

also known as Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India" and a beloved President.

Career & Contributions

He played a leading role in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts. He worked with "Defence Research and Development Organisation"DRDO and title "Indian Space Research Organisation"ISRO.

Selected achievements

  • Project director for SLV-III
  • Key contributions to missile programs: Agni, Prithvi
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna

Incorrect earlier statement about his birth year. Corrected: Born in 1931.

Terms & Definitions

Missile Man
A nickname for his work in missile development.
People's President
Affectionate title reflecting his popularity across India.
